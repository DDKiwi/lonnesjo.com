Daniel Violin Lönnesjö

Artist based in Haverdal, Sweden.

Working across painting, drawing, and sculpture, Daniel’s practice is intuitive and energetic, creating work that feels spontaneous, physical, and alive.

He is drawn to large-scale compositions where figuration meets abstraction and where painting, drawing, and sculptural thinking intersect. Bold gestures, layered surfaces, and dynamic forms explore movement, tension, and emotional presence.

Experimentation drives his work. He combines oil, enamel, spray paint, and found objects across two- and three-dimensional pieces. Rather than seeking perfection, he focuses on capturing feeling—allowing honesty and imperfection to shape each work.
